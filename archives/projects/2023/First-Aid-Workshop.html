First Aid Workshop

Projects - 2023

Project Objective

  • Equip young Royalists with practical skills to effectively respond to various emergency situations.
  • Increase participants' awareness of basic health and safety practices beyond first aid.
  • Instill a sense of responsibility and empowerment in participants to act as first responders, bridging gaps in immediate medical assistance.
  • Creating a platform for students to network with healthcare professionals and peers, facilitating the exchange of knowledge and experiences related to first aid and emergency response.
  • To train students on the correct methods of providing first aid to casualties.
  • To uplift the healthcare system of Sri Lanka.

Project Overview

12th of July, 2023

8.00am - 11.00am

MAS Arena, Royal College

Agenda

8.00am - Welcome Address by Nafees Salley (Club Chairman)

8.10am - Introduction about International Red Cross and Red Crescent Federation

8.20am - Introduction to the Guest Speaker (Dr. Suhail Shukry)

8.25am - First Aid in General

9.15am - How to Respond during emergencies and critical conditions

9.45am - Vote of Thanks by Althaf Isfahan (Club Treasurer)

10.00am - Conclusion

The "First Aid Workshop" project, conducted by Dr. Suhail Shukry, was an initiative by the Royal College Red Cross Society to teach basic first aid to its club members. Dr. Shukry, a renowned expert in the field of healthcare, delivered a comprehensive workshop that covered various dimensions of first aid.

Dr. Shukry's speech transcended conventional first aid training. Participants were guided on how to respond to a spectrum of situations, including sports injuries, mental health crises, and various types of diseases. The workshop provided a clear understanding of healthcare, emphasizing preventive measures to stay away from health challenges.
