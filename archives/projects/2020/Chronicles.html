Projects - 2020

Project Objectives

  • For students to understand how the pandemic crisis affects different sorts of lives and how the professionals handle it.
  • For students to get professional advice on how to prevent, manage, and overcome the novel Coronavirus.
  • Provide a platform for frontline workers, administrators, and policymakers to share their experiences with students.
  • Get professionals from various fields of expertise to guide us through the different aspects of life during this new paradigm we are facing.
  • Give an opportunity for students to come up with ideas on how they too can contribute to the eradication of Covid-19 from our country.
  • An opportunity for the older generation to teach the younger generation how to navigate through the new normal life we are living now.

Project Overview

Due to the virus and the long lockdown that was experienced throughout the country, the students around Sri Lanka are in confusion as to what to do to stay safe from this deadly virus. This is a project organized by the Royal College Red Cross Society who seeks to give a platform to all frontline workers, administrators, and policymakers, who are working hard to curb the novel Covid-19, a chance to share their experiences directly to students, depending on their expertise and what we can do to prevent it to keep our country safe.

Project Details

Royal College Red Cross Society created a platform for all frontline workers, administrators, and policymakers, who are working hard to curb the novel COVID 19 virus, a chance to share their experiences directly to students, depending on their expertise and what we can do to prevent it to keep our country safe.

Also, we interviewed prominent businessmen and artists to share their thought and opinion on the current situation in the country.
