About RCRCS

Royal College Red Cross

Founded in 1978, under the guidance of Mr. S.J. Maranthota, the Royal College Red Cross Society remains to this day as one of the most respected and important clubs of the College, providing a great service to the school and to the community. It is now a unit of the Sri Lanka Red Cross Society.

Founding Members:

  • S.G.D. Dias (President)
  • S. Peiris (Secretary)
  • C.L.P. Kumarage (Treasurer)
  • A.S. Sideeque
  • S. Mayudeen
  • P. Kuruppu

Major Projects:

  • 1978 – Assisting for the release campaign for the eastern province cyclone victims.
  • 1979 – Arranging the first eye donation campaign which was organized for the first time by a school.
  • 1979 – Organizing the first blood donation campaign.
  • Supplying first aid for every major event in Royal College.

We have proudly continued this legacy and held the 44st Annual Blood Donation Campaign on 2023. We are an important supplier to the National Blood Bank, providing them with more than 540 pints of blood, the largest quantity collected from any school in Sri Lanka as of 2023.
