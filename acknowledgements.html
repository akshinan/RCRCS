BUILT BY BOYS IN THE SCHOOL

The phrase "Built by boys in the school" is inspired by The History of Royal College, Written by Boys in the School, a book co-authored by 20 students in 1931. This tradition of encouraging initiative among students is alive and well at Royal College, and the Royal College Red Cross Society website is a venture that signifies the continuity of this phrase.

The current version of royalcollegeredcross.com & poe.royalcollegeredcross.com are results of the effort of the members of the Royal College Red Cross Society.
