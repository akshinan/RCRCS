Infusion'23

Projects - 2023

Project Objectives

  • Provide support and assistance to individuals and communities facing various challenges such as poverty, hunger, health issues, education and disaster relief.
  • Raise awareness about specific social issues and causes. Advocate for policy changes/social reforms to address those issues at the systemic level.
  • Empower individuals and communities by providing them with tools, resource, and opportunities to improve wellbeing, build resilience, develop skills, and achieve self-sufficiency.
  • Evaluate the impact and effectiveness of their intervention, measure outcomes, and ensure outcomes and ensure long term sustainability of their initiative.
  • Giving a chance for the young royalists to enhance the lives of others during these trying times and educate them about the importance of community service and social responsibility.
  • Setting a good example to our club members and also to the general public.
  • Cultivating Values of Compassion and Empathy by promoting values such as compassion, empathy, and a sense of social responsibility among royalists and the wider community.

Project Overview

Phase 1 - Humanity'23

23rd April 2023

Around Colombo Area

150 Parcels

The Royal College Red Cross Society alongside the Leo Club of Royal College organized a phase of the project Infusion named Humanity’23 on April 23rd 2023. Food parcels were distributed among the ones in need in the Colombo Areas. The parcels consisted of local delicacies such as Kokis, Milk Rice, Sour plantains and etc. The project aims to promote compassion, solidarity, and social responsibility among students and volunteers of Royal College.

Phase 2 - Hydrate’23

12th November 2023

Anuradhapura, Sri Lanka

A Community Service project by the Globe Family of Royal College, aimed at satisfying the needs of the many children who live in and around Anuradhapura who require purified water due to the high water pollution. The Royal College Red Cross Society will be installing them on behalf of the Globe Family.

We used our contacts in Anuradhapura through the Blood Donation Campaign, we conducted there to find schools in need and to install the filters in the schools

14 water filters were be donated to 7 schools in Anuradhapura.

Phase 3 - Aram’23

21st November 2023

Sri Jinananda Childrens Home, Wellawatte

The Royal College Red Cross Society alongside the Hindu Students Union is organizing a phase of the project Infusion named Aram’23. We aim to collect donations from young Royalists and donate it to a children’s home in Colombo. The project aims to promote compassion, solidarity, and social responsibility among students and volunteers of Royal College.
