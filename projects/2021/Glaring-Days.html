Glaring Days

Projects - 2021

Project Objectives

  • To educate people about the importance of international days. The 23 posters shared across Instagram, Facebook, and WhatsApp were able to educate people on various important topics, especially World Suicide Prevention Day, World Mental Health Day, and many other international days.
  • Most international or global days are celebrated to create awareness and educate the community about topics of concern that need global attention. We were able to contribute to this through the 23 posters shared across social media platforms.
  • To raise awareness among younger generations on international days. Many kids in the younger generation are not aware of these days and their importance, but we were able to educate them through this project.
